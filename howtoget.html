How to get here

My office 2.O7.114 is located in Building NO on Campus Plaine. Once in the building, go to the 7th floor and search for the corridor "Géométrie différentielle" and search for the door with my name on it on your right.

If you arrive to the central station ("Bruxelles Central"), take Bus 71 in the direction Delta and disembark at the stop Fraiteur, which is marked by '2' on the map of Campus Plaine. From there, Building NO is within a 3min. walk.

If you arrive to the south station ("Bruxelles Midi"), take the metro Line 2 in the direction ELISABETH. Change at Arts-Loi to Line 5 in the direction HERRMANN-DEBROUX. Disembark at Delta and take the exit ULB, which is marked by '4' on the map of Campus Plaine. From there, Building NO is within a 5min. walk.

For the bus and metro schedule consult STIB.

Airports

Brussels has a mid-sized airport, which is well connected with the city. However, if you are coming from far away, there may well be no (direct) flight to Brussels. Large airports which are reasonably well connected by train with Brussels are Amsterdam Schipol, Paris Charles de Gaulle, and Frankfurt.
